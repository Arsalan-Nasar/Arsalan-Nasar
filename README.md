### 🧭 Who I Am

I'm **Arsalan Nasar**, founder and CEO of **ZIRA Technologies** — an AI company built for the
real world. I design and build **AI products that solve real problems**, starting with the
markets I understand best: **law and education in Pakistan**. I take ideas from concept to
shipped product — architecture, engineering, design, and strategy.

> **Our name comes from the Arabic word for *seed*** — because in every field we enter, we
> plant intelligence from the ground up.

---

### 🏢 ZIRA Technologies — *Where Intelligence Begins*

We build AI tools for professional industries — **intelligent, verifiable, and built for the
people who depend on them most.** We start where the need is most acute and the tools least
available. Pakistani professionals deserve world-class AI — and we build it here in Quetta,
designed to scale globally.

🌐 **[getzira.tech](https://getzira.tech)**
